What Does "Percent" Mean?

The word "percent" literally means "per one hundred." It's a way to express a fraction or a ratio where the whole is always considered to be 100. This provides a standard way to compare different quantities.

%

The symbol for percentage, representing a value out of 100.

1. Score to Percentage

To turn a score or fraction into a percentage, simply multiply it by 100. This scales the fraction to be out of 100.

(Score / Total) Ã— 100 = Percentage

An exam score of 80 out of 120 is equivalent to 66.66%.

2. Percentage to Fraction

To convert a percentage back to a fraction, place the percentage value over a denominator of 100 and simplify.

Percentage / 100 = Equivalent Fraction

10% is the same as the fraction 10/100, which simplifies to 1/10.

3. Fraction to Decimal

When the denominator is a power of 10 (like 100), count the zeros. Move the numerator's decimal point to the left by that many places.

Question 1: Convert 45% into a simplified fraction and a decimal.

Solution:

  • Step 1, Fraction Setup, Write the percentage over 100, which gives 45 / 100.
  • Step 2, Simplify Fraction, Divide both the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor, 5. This simplifies the fraction to 9 / 20.
  • Step 3, Decimal Conversion, To convert 45 / 100 to a decimal, move the decimal point two places to the left, since there are two zeros in 100.
  • Answer, The simplified fraction is 9 / 20, and the decimal is 0.45.

Question 2: In a class of 50 students, 35 passed the exam. What percentage of students passed?

Solution:

  • Step 1, Form the Fraction, The ratio of passed students to total students is 35 / 50.
  • Step 2, Convert to Percentage, To convert any fraction to a percentage, multiply it by 100. Write it as (35 / 50) multiplied by 100.
  • Step 3, Calculate, Simplify the calculation, which is 0.7 multiplied by 100, which equals 70.
  • Answer, 70% of the students passed the exam.
