Student Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Specially designed for students and parents. This policy explains how we protect student data and ensure a safe learning environment.

1. For Students & Parents

Dear Students

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities. We've designed Padhle Champs to help you learn effectively while keeping your personal information secure. We never share your data with advertisers or sell it to anyone.

Your data is encrypted and secure
Only you and your teachers can see your progress
No advertisements or third-party tracking

Dear Parents

We understand your concerns about your child's online safety. Padhle Champs is designed with strict privacy controls and educational focus. You have full visibility and control over your child's account.

Monitor your child's learning progress
Control privacy settings and permissions
Direct contact with our support team

We are committed to creating a safe, educational environment where students can learn and grow without privacy concerns.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

Name, email, and phone number
Class level and batch enrollment (Sangharsh, Manzil, Vigyan, CodeCrack)
Profile picture (optional)
Parent/guardian contact information

Learning Data

Test scores and DPP performance
XP points and learning streaks
Video lesson progress and completion
Subject-wise performance analytics

Technical Data

Device type and browser information
Login times and session duration
Firebase authentication data
Security logs and access patterns

Platform Interactions

Doubt submissions and teacher responses
Eduvia AI chat interactions
Community posts and creative corner submissions
Notification preferences and delivery status

3. How We Use Your Information

Learning Enhancement

Batch-Specific Content

Deliver appropriate content for Sangharsh, Manzil, Vigyan, and CodeCrack batches

XP & Streak Tracking

Calculate experience points and maintain learning streaks for motivation

AI-Powered Assistance

Provide personalized help through Eduvia AI based on your learning patterns

Platform Operations

Performance Analytics

Generate test analysis, DPP reports, and progress dashboards

Smart Notifications

Send batch-specific updates, test reminders, and DPP notifications

Community Features

Enable leaderboards, creative corner, and peer interactions
