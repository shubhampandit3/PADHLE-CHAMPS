Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" on the homepage, enter your details, and verify your email. Parents need to approve accounts for students under 13.

Which batch should I choose?

Sangharsh/Manzil: Class 5 comprehensive learning
Vigyan: Class 10 science focus
CodeCrack: Programming for Classes 7-12

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.

Can I use Padhle Champs on my phone?

Yes! Our platform works on all devices - phones, tablets, and computers. Just use your web browser for the best experience.

Learning Features

How do XP points and streaks work?

Earn XP by completing lessons, tests, and DPPs. Maintain daily learning to build your streak. Check the leaderboard to see your ranking!

What are DPPs and how do I solve them?

DPPs (Daily Practice Problems) are daily questions to test your understanding. Find them in your dashboard and submit answers before the deadline.

How do I use Eduvia AI assistant?

Click on "Eduvia AI" from your dashboard. Ask questions about any subject, get study tips, or request explanations of difficult concepts.

How do I watch video lessons?

Go to your batch page, select a subject, then choose a chapter. Click on any video to start watching. You can pause, rewind, and take notes.

Tests & Analytics

How do timed tests work?

Tests have a fixed time limit. Once you start, the timer begins. Answer all questions and submit before time runs out. Auto-submit happens at the end.

How do I view my test analysis?

Go to "Test Analysis" from your dashboard to see detailed performance reports, subject-wise scores, and improvement suggestions.

What is the test leaderboard?

The leaderboard shows top performers in tests and overall XP. It updates in real-time and helps you track your progress against peers.

Can I download my progress reports?

Yes! Visit your Progress Dashboard to download detailed reports of your performance, which you can share with parents or teachers.

Community & Support

How do I ask doubts to teachers?

Click "Ask Doubts" from your dashboard, select the subject, type your question, and submit. Teachers will respond within 24 hours.

What is the Creative Corner?

Share your creative work like poems, art, projects, and stories with the community. Get feedback and appreciate others' creativity.

How do Interactive Notes work?

Create smart notes with AI assistance, organize them by subjects, and use them for quick revision. Notes sync across all your devices.

How do I report technical issues?

Use "Report Issues" from your dashboard to submit technical problems. Include details about what happened and we'll fix it quickly.

For Parents & Guardians

How can I monitor my child's progress?

Create a parent account linked to your child's profile. You'll receive weekly progress reports and can view their dashboard anytime.

Is the platform safe for my child?

Absolutely! We follow strict safety protocols, have no advertisements, moderate all content, and comply with child privacy laws.

What notifications will I receive?

Get updates about your child's test scores, XP milestones, streak achievements, and any important announcements from teachers.

How do I contact my child's teacher?

Use the "Contact Teacher" feature in your parent dashboard, or email directly through the platform's messaging system.
